A drone, also known as an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV), is an aircraft that is operated without a human pilot on board. Drones can be remotely controlled by a human operator or autonomously programmed to fly on predefined routes or perform specific tasks.

Drones come in various shapes, sizes, and configurations, ranging from small quadcopters used for recreational purposes to large fixed-wing aircraft employed for military surveillance or cargo delivery. They can be equipped with a wide range of sensors, cameras, and other payloads to collect data, capture images or videos, conduct surveillance, monitor environmental conditions, or even deliver goods.
